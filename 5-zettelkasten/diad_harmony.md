### Diad Harmony
- **Diad** harmony combines two colors that are two steps apart on the color wheel.

#### Formula
- Diad color schemes use a combination consisting of two colors set `30°` apart on the color wheel.
	- `H1 = |(H0 + 30°) - 360°`
