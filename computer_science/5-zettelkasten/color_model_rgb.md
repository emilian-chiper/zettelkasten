### The RGB Color Model
- *Colors:* red, green, blue.
- *Type:* additive.
- *Input Devices:*
	- image scanners,
	- video games,
	- digital cameras,
	- television,
	- video cameras.
- *Output devices*:
	- television-set technologies,
	- video projector systems,
	- computer and mobile phone displays.
- *Formula:* Bl + R + G + B = Wh.
