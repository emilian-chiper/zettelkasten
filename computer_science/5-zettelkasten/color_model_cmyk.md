### The CMYK Color Model
- *Colors:* cyan, magenta, yellow, black.
- *Type:* subtractive.
- *Output devices:*
	- color inkjet,
	- laser,
	- dye-sublimation printers.
- *Formula:* Wh + C + M + Y = Bl (subtractive, i.e. Wh = Bl - C - M - Y).
